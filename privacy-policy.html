Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Invoice Builder ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you visit our website at mohdabdullah46345-ops.github.io/Invoice-Builder- (the "Site"). By using the Site, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information we collect

We aim to collect as little personal data as possible.

  • Invoice data you enter: Any details you type into the invoice generator (company name, client details, items, prices, logo, etc.) are processed entirely within your web browser. This information is never sent to or stored on our servers.
  • Local storage: To make your experience convenient, the Site saves your current invoice draft in your browser's local storage so you can return to it later. You can clear this at any time using the "Reset" button or by clearing your browser data.
  • Contact information: If you email us, we receive the information you choose to provide (such as your name, email address and message).

2. Cookies and similar technologies

The Site itself does not set tracking cookies. However, third-party services we use (see below) may set cookies on your device to provide their functionality, measure performance, and serve relevant advertising.

3. Third-party advertising (Google AdSense)

We may display advertisements served by Google AdSense. In doing so:

  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our Site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • You can learn more about how Google uses data from sites that use its services at policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

Third-party vendors may also use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits. You can opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalised advertising by visiting aboutads.info/choices.

4. Analytics

We may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand general usage trends (such as page views). Any such data is aggregated and is not used to identify individual visitors.

5. How we use information

  • To operate and improve the Site and its features.
  • To respond to your enquiries when you contact us.
  • To display advertising that helps keep the tool free.

6. Data retention

Invoice drafts stored in local storage remain on your own device until you clear them. We do not retain copies. Emails you send us are kept only as long as needed to address your request.

7. Children's privacy

The Site is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Your rights

Because we do not store your invoice data on our servers, you remain in full control of it. For any data you have shared with us by email, you may request access or deletion by contacting us.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at mohdabdullah46345@gmail.com or via our Contact page.
