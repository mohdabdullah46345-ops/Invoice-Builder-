About Invoice Builder

Last updated: June 2026

Invoice Builder is a free, browser-based invoice generator that helps freelancers, consultants and small business owners create clean, professional invoices in just a few minutes — without signing up or installing any software.

Our mission

Billing should be simple. Many small businesses lose time wrestling with spreadsheets or paying monthly fees for tools they barely use. We built Invoice Builder to give everyone a fast, no-cost way to generate a polished invoice and download it as a PDF, right from the web browser.

What makes it different

  • Completely free. There are no subscriptions, trials or hidden charges.
  • Privacy first. Your invoice data is processed entirely in your browser and saved only on your own device using local storage. We do not upload or store your invoices on any server.
  • No account needed. Open the page and start typing — that is all it takes.
  • Professional output. Add your logo, choose a currency, apply tax and discounts, and export a print-ready PDF.

Who built it

Invoice Builder is created and maintained by Abdullah, a developer who wanted a lightweight tool for sending invoices without the overhead of heavy accounting platforms. The project began as a single-page tool and has grown into a small resource hub with practical guides on invoicing and getting paid.
