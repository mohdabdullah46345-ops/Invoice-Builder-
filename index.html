How to create an invoice

  1. Enter your company name, contact details and (optionally) upload your logo.
  2. Add the client's billing details under Bill To.
  3. Set an invoice number, issue date and due date.
  4. Add each product or service as a line item with quantity and price.
  5. Apply tax and/or a discount if needed — the total updates automatically.
  6. Click Download PDF to save your invoice, or print it directly.
